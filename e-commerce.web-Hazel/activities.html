Live Music Nights

Enjoy the soothing sounds of live music while sipping your favorite coffee. We host regular live music nights featuring local artists and bands. Check our schedule for upcoming performances.

Expand your coffee knowledge and palate by attending our coffee tasting events. Our experts will guide you through different coffee flavors, brewing methods, and origins. It's a great opportunity to learn and discover new favorites.

Immerse yourself in the world of art with our rotating art exhibitions. We showcase works from local artists, providing a unique and inspiring ambiance. Grab a coffee and appreciate the creativity on display.
